Certain 6. Radius of Curvature 15 min
Q (2022 and 2023 Group B): Find radius of curvature at any point (x,y) for the curve y²=4ax
Steps: dy/dx = 2a/y → d²y/dx² = -4a²/y³ → ρ = [1+(2a/y)²]^(3/2) / (-4a²/y³) → ρ = (y²+4a²)^(3/2) / 4a²
Q (2025 Group B): Find radius of curvature at any point of x=a(θ+sinθ), y=a(1-cosθ)

  • Coordinate system: Cartesian↔Polar: x=rcosθ, y=rsinθ, r=√(x²+y²), θ=tan⁻¹(y/x). Spherical: x=r sinφ cosθ, y=r sinφ sinθ, z=r cosφ. Cylindrical: x=r cosθ, y=r sinθ, z=z
